Baby Back Ribs

Description

Tender, slow-cooked baby back ribs coated and oven-cooked in barbeque sauce.

Ingredients

  • 3 pounds of baby back ribs, trimmed
  • salt and ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/2 cup of water
  • 1/2 an onion, sliced
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 18oz barbeque sauce

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Season ribs with salt and pepper.
  3. Pour the water into the slow cooker, then add the ribs. Scatter the onion and garlic over top. Cover and cook on low for 8 hours or high for 4 hours.
  4. When the ribs are almost finished, preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).
  5. Transfer ribs from the slow cooker to a baking sheet and discard the onion and garlic. Coat the ribs with the barbeque sauce.
  6. Finally, bake the ribs in the preheated oven until sauce caramelizes and sticks to meat, 10 to 15 minutes.
