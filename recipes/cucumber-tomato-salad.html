Cucumber and Tomato Salad

Description

A refreshing cucumber and tomato salad with seasonings, onion, and a sweet and tangy vinaigrette.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup of water
  • 1/2 a cup of distilled white vinegar
  • 1/4 of a cup of vegetable oil
  • 1/4 of a cup of sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of salt, or to taste
  • 1 teaspoon of freshly groun black pepper, or to taste
  • 3 cucumbers, peeled and sliced 1/4 of an inch thick
  • 3 tomatoes. cut into wedges
  • 1 onion, sliced and seperated into rings

Steps

  1. Gather all the ingredients.
  2. Whisk the water, vinegar, salt, and pepper together in a large bowl until the mixture is smooth.
  3. Add the cucumbers, tomatoes, and onions, then stir to coat them in the mixture.
  4. Cover the bowl with a plastic wrap, and refrigerate for at least two hours before serving.
