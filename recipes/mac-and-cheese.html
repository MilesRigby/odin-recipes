Mac and Cheese

Description

An easyto make, creamy, slow-cooked mac and cheese.

Ingredients

  • Macaroni
  • A stick of butter
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Cheddar cheese, shredded
  • Milk; whole and evaporated
  • Two eggs, beaten
  • Can of cheddar cheese soup
  • Ground paprika (optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta in salted water, then drain and transfer to the slow cooker.
  2. Stir in the butter, seasonings, and about half of the Cheddar.
  3. Whisk the evaporated milk and eggs together, then stir into the pasta.
  4. Whisk the whole milk and soup together, then stir into the pasta.
  5. Sprinkle the remaining cheese over the pasta mixture and garnish with paprika.
  6. Cook in a slow cooker on low for 2 Â½ to 3 hours.
